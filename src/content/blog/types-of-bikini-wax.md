# Different Types of Bikini Wax | A Comprehensive Guide

Discover the various **types of bikini wax** available, from basic to daring. Learn about the different options and choose what's best for you.

## Types of Bikini Wax — Which One Is Right for You?

Bikini waxing is a process of removing unwanted hair from the genital area. It often involves the removal of all visible pubic hair, as well as hair from the inner thighs and buttocks (depending on the desired outcome). The bikini wax should only be done when the **skin is clean and dry**, and it is important to **prepare your skin before waxing** to reduce irritation and discomfort.

**Important:** Before undergoing any kind of bikini waxing, it is best to consult with a doctor or dermatologist as they can advise you on what type of procedure would be right for you.

## Basic Bikini Wax

A **basic bikini wax** is the most minimal option, perfect for those new to waxing or wanting simple maintenance.

### What It Covers

- Removes hair **outside the panty line**
- Focuses on the **sides** of the bikini area
- Leaves most pubic hair intact

### Advantages

- **Quick and easy** — Usually takes **10-15 minutes**
- **Cost-effective** — Typically ranges from **$25-$40**
- **Less discomfort** than more extensive waxing options
- Great for **first-time waxers**
- Provides a **basic level of grooming**
- Good option for those who want to remove excess hair around the bikini line but don't want full hair removal

### Who Should Choose This

- Waxing beginners
- Those wanting minimal hair removal
- Budget-conscious clients
- People with **low pain tolerance**

## French Bikini Wax

A **French bikini wax** (also known as a **landing strip**) removes more hair than a basic bikini wax while leaving a strip of hair on the pubic bone.

### What It Covers

- Removes hair from the **sides and top** of the pubic area
- Removes hair from the **labia**
- Leaves a **strip or triangle** of hair on the pubic bone
- Does **not** include the buttocks area

### Advantages

- **Longer-lasting results** than shaving — Hair removed from the root takes **3-4 weeks** to grow back
- **More thorough hair removal** — Can remove even very short hairs
- Helps **prevent ingrown hairs** compared to shaving
- Leaves skin **feeling smoother**
- More defined shape than basic bikini wax

### Who Should Choose This

- Those wanting more definition than a basic wax
- Clients who prefer leaving some hair
- People wanting a **neat, maintained look**

## Hollywood Wax (Full Brazilian)

A **Hollywood wax** removes **all hair** from the pubic area, front to back.

### What It Covers

- Removes **all hair** on the pubic area (front)
- Includes the **labia**
- Includes **between the buttocks** (the "butt strip")
- Leaves the area **completely bare**

### Advantages

- **Smooth and clean feeling** — No stubble or irritation like shaving
- **Longest-lasting results** of all bikini wax types
- **Complete hair removal** for those who prefer no hair
- Perfect for **swimwear** of any style
- No maintenance needed for **3-6 weeks**

### Considerations

- More **intense sensation** than other types
- Takes **15-30 minutes**
- Costs **$50-$100+**
- May require **more frequent appointments** initially

## Extended Bikini Wax

An **extended bikini wax** is a middle ground between a basic bikini wax and a Brazilian.

### What It Covers

- Removes hair **deeper inside the panty line** than a basic wax
- Removes hair from the **top** of the pubic area (optional)
- Does **not** include the labia or buttocks
- Can be customized based on preference

### Advantages

- **Cleaner look** than basic bikini wax
- Great for **high-cut swimsuits** or underwear
- **Less discomfort** than a French or Brazilian wax
- Removes more hair than basic but not as much as full Brazilian
- Customizable based on your preferences

### Who Should Choose This

- Those wanting more than basic but less than Brazilian
- People who wear **high-cut styles**
- Clients wanting to **ease into** more extensive waxing

## High Bikini Wax

A **high bikini wax** focuses on the sides and is perfect for thong wearers.

### What It Covers

- Removes hair from the **upper thighs**
- Extends further **inward** than a basic wax
- Focuses on the **sides** of the bikini area
- Does not touch the top or labia area

### Advantages

- **Clean and polished look** around the bikini area
- **Perfect for thong wearers** — Removes all hair that would be visible
- Neat and tidy appearance
- Great for **summer swimwear**
- Less extensive than full Brazilian

### Who Should Choose This

- Thong or high-cut bikini wearers
- Those wanting side definition only
- People wanting a **polished, neat appearance**

## Choosing the Right Bikini Wax for You

Consider these factors when deciding:

- **Your comfort level** — Start with basic if you're nervous
- **Your budget** — Prices vary significantly
- **Your pain tolerance** — More extensive = more sensation
- **Your lifestyle** — Swimwear, activities, preferences
- **Your maintenance goals** — How often you want appointments

Both **hard wax** and **soft wax** get the job done by removing the hair from the root, allowing the skin to stay smooth longer than shaving. By pulling the hair from the root, it allows more time for the new hair to grow in soft.

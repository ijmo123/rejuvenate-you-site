## Is It Safe to Wax During Your Period?
Yes, it is perfectly safe to get waxed during your period. However, there are a few factors you might want to consider before booking your appointment. Firstly, skin sensitivity tends to increase the few days before menstruation and sometimes till the first few days of your period due to hormonal changes. This can result in a more painful waxing experience than usual. Additionally, because your body is already going through a lot during this time, you might experience heightened discomfort.

## Best Types of Wax for Menstrual Waxing
For menstrual waxing, hard wax is the recommended option due to its several advantages, especially for sensitive skin that may be more delicate during your period. Hard wax uniquely adheres only to the hair, not the skin, which significantly reduces discomfort and the risk of irritation or redness. This feature is particularly beneficial during menstruation when the skin may be more sensitive. Additionally, hard wax is effective at removing shorter hairs, which means fewer applications and less chance of irritating the skin, making it an ideal choice for a comfortable waxing experience.

## Tips for a Smooth Waxing Experience During Your Period

- Schedule Wisely: If possible, schedule your waxing session for the least sensitive days of your period, typically the later days.
- Freshen Up: Prepare for your waxing with a clean tampon or a menstrual cup. Usually cleaning wipes and tampons are provided in our restrooms.
- Pain Management: Consider taking an over-the-counter pain reliever about 30 minutes before your appointment to ease discomfort.
- Wear Comfortable Clothing: Loose clothing can reduce irritation after waxing, providing comfort as your skin calms.
